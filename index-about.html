About Us

At Honey's Homebakes, we bring passion and care into every loaf and pastry we create. With years of expertise and the finest ingredients, we’re here to make your day sweeter.

At the heart of Honey's Homebakes are Rojani and Tirso Abeleda, the creative duo turning everyday baking into an art form. With Rojani’s passion for crafting delightful treats and Tirso’s innovative touch, they’ve built more than just a bakery—they’ve created a canvas for edible masterpieces. Every bite is a reflection of their shared commitment to quality, artistry, and the joy of savoring something truly special. Using only the finest ingredients, they pour their hearts into every creation, ensuring that each treat brings a little sweetness and a lot of love to your day.

Tirso Abeleda

Tirso Abeleda is the artistic soul of Honey's Homebakes. As the visionary behind each design, he transforms baked goods into edible masterpieces. With his eye for detail and flair for creativity, Tirso infuses every delicacy with unique themes and stunning artistry, proving that Honey's Homebakes offers more than just desserts—it delivers true works of art.

Rojani Abeleda

Our founder, Rojani Abeleda, is the heart and soul behind Honey's Homebakes. With a passion for baking and a commitment to quality, Rojani has been creating delicious baked goods that bring smiles to all our customers. Every treat is made with care and love, using the finest ingredients to create sweet memories for everyone.
